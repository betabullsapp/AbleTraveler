    About

    As a child I lived in several countries in Europe, but I mainly grew up in Belgium and then my family moved to Chicago. My mother loved to travel and had been all over Europe and the Middle East in the 1930’s – setting the tone for my future in the industry.I love working with people on their travel, whether they are trying to plan a vacation, set up a family reunion, or take a routine business trip. It’s rewarding to find precisely what a client hopes for, especially if they don’t think it’s possible. I find it highly rewarding to solve any travel dilemma.
